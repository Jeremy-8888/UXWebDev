Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need to join?

For beginners, the club provides basic equipment including airsoft guns, protective eyewear, and face masks. As you progress, you may choose to purchase your own equipment. We'll provide guidance on recommended gear.

Is airsoft safe?

Airsoft is definitely safe and we ensure it in our club. Safety is our top priority. All participants must wear protective eyewear and follow strict safety protocols. Our experienced instructors ensure all activities are conducted safely with proper supervision.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is necessary! We welcome complete beginners and provide comprehensive training on safety, equipment handling, and tactical basics. Our experienced members and intstructors are always happy to help newcomers.

What activities does the club organize?

We organize 2 weekly training sessions and 1 Monthly Tournament these events may be paused in exam and ICA periods. Other than that, we may plan non-recurring events such as participating in external Tournaments, team-building and bonding activities, and booths at school events (National day, Student-Life Carnival, etc)

How much does it cost to join?

Membership is free, there is a subsidised fee for participating in Tournaments. Internal and External trainings are free and you can borrow and use club equipment free of charge. However, bb pellets are not provided. Additionally if you wish to purchase airsoft equipment we can provide discounts through our partners.
