About Our Club

Founded in 2015, the NYP Airsoft Club is a dynamic student-led community dedicated to tactical training, teamwork, and discipline through safe airsoft activities. Any student with an interest in airsoft is welcomed! We host weekly drills, scenario-based games, and inter-school tournaments. Join us to sharpen your mind and reflexes!

Our Objectives

Safety First

Promote safe handling and operation of airsoft equipment with certified training.

Teamwork

Develop communication and coordination skills through team-based scenarios.

Strategy

Enhance critical thinking and problem-solving abilities through tactical games.

Sportsmanship

Foster respect, integrity and fair play among all participants.
