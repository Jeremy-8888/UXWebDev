About Our Club

Welcome to NYP Airsoft. We are an airsoft enthusiasts club in Nanynag Polytechnic with a deep passion for tactical airsoft play and realistic fun scenarios. However, we are more than just a CCA we are a family that supports and uplifts each other through airsoft and camaraderie. Every week, our dedicated members push their limits in tactical drills, realistic battlefield simulations, and heart-pounding tournaments, constantly honing their skills while fostering leadership and camaraderie. Whether you're a seasoned operator or holding a replica for the first time, our community welcomes all who share our fiery enthusiasm for this electrifying sport.
